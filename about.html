About Me

My Story

When I was little I always loved being on computers. Whether that was surfing the internet, making presentations for school, or playing games, I always knew I wanted to do something with computers. When I was in middle school, I discovered coding and instantly knew that I wanted my career to be in Computer Science, no matter what specialty. In high school I learned my first language, Python. Writing Python affirmed that I did in fact want a career in this field, but writing to a console didn't feel right. When I learned HTML and CSS in college, I loved being able to visually see what I was coding, and decided to pursue a career in Web Development.

Gaming naturally became my favorite hobby, and gave me a topic to use when exploring various aspects of coding, such as APIs. When my head isn't buried in my computer, I attempt to wear out my 3 infinite energy dogs. They also decide when I've spent too much time online for the day, coercing me with barking and playtime. I've always been a dog lover, even making it my job through college while supporting myself.
